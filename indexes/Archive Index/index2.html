Meet The Bois

Marceau Dauboin - The current owner of The Yeastie Bois. A half French, half South African 18 year old boy from Table View. He has a great love for good food, health and of course delicious bread... This is why none of our products contain added sugar, preservatives and other unecessary ingredients.


The Yeastie Bois Mk1 - The Original Yeastie Bois matured starter. Using nothing but stoneground unbleached Rye Flour and Water, the yeast was originally fermented over a period of 7 days and has been regularily fed using the same ingredients ever since, being the mother to all others yeasts.




The Yeastie Bois Mk2 - Much larger than the Mk1, now used for larger orders.

Meet The Bois

Marceau Dauboin - The current owner of The Yeastie Bois. A half French, half South African 18 year old boy from Table View. He has a great love for good food, health and of course delicious bread... This is why none of our products contain added sugar, preservatives and other unecessary ingredients.

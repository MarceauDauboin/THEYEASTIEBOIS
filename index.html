Meet The Bois

MARCEAU DAUBOIN - The owner of The Yeastie Bois. A half French, half South African 18 year old from Table View. He has a great love for good food, health and of course delicious bread... This is why none of our products contain added sugar, preservatives and other unecessary ingredients.

THE Yeastie BOIS MK1 - The Original Yeastie Bois matured starter(yeast culture). Using nothing but stoneground unbleached Rye Flour and Water, the yeast was originally fermented over a period of 7 days and has been regularily fed using the same ingredients ever since, being the mother to all others yeasts.

THE Yeastie BOIS MK2 - Much larger than the Mk1, now used for larger orders.
